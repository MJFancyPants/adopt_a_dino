Care and Feeding of Your Pet Dino

Once you've adopted a new dinosaur, it's very important to give it the proper care and food. Here are some useful tips for living with your new Mesozoic friend.

Dino Care Tip

Don't let your dinosaur watch too much TV. Many carnivorous dinosaurs are attracted to the movement on the screen. They may end up fascinated for hours and forget to eat or exercise. Or, worse yet, they may mistake the TV for prey and attack it.
